The Evolution of Pop Art Design

Pop Art began in the 1950s and 60s with artists like Andy Warhol and Roy Lichtenstein. Over time, it evolved from traditional painting to digital media, influencing fashion, packaging, and graphic design. Today, its bold colors and patterns remain symbols of creativity and rebellion.
